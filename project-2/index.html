How to properly pour a bowl of cereal

Cereal has brought more joy to my life then anything else and I just want to share that love with you all. It doesn't matter the time of day, cereal can be consumed any time. It must be poured properly to get the most out of it.

Ingredients

  1. 1 box of Cereal of choice (My favorites include Lucky Charms, Fruit Loops, and Frosted Flakes)

  2. 1 gallon of milk (after learning how to properly pour a bowl, you'll go through a whole gallon fast)

  3. 1 pinch of sugar

    4. !!Optional: 3 fresh strawberries


    Instructions

    Step one Make sure that your fridge temperature is set at 38.8 degrees farenheit. This is on the warmer side but I promise the cereal will taste at it's best on the warmer side. (note: this is not required but certainly helps with flavor) .
    Step two While you are waiting for the milk to get right, put the bowl you will be using in the freezer to chill.(Don't leave it in for over 10 minutes or it could break upon removal easier)
    Step three This next part is key. There is quite a debate in the cereal world about whether the cereal or the milk goes first and I am here to tell you it shouldn't be a debate. The cereal goes first!!!!
    Step four Next, pour the milk over the cereal. It is vitally important to not oversaturate nor undersaturate the cereal with the milk. The rule of thumb for me is halfway up the amount of cereal you poured.
    Step five *optional* Dice the fresh straberries up into 1 inch pieces and add to the bowl. Mix in gently with the milk and cereal.
    Step six ENJOY
